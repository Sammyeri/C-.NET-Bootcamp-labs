My Journey

My journey into coding was a sudden one. I originally went to college to learn about biomedical engineering, but wasnt quite happy with it.

Part of the degree program was to take an engineering class that included learning to use MATLab. this was just the surface of coding for me.

I decided to take a break from college and was made aware of Grand Circus and an upcoming bootcamp. I looked it up and it looked intresting so in the summer of 2022 I started the Full-Stack JavaScript Bootcamp

I spent the next 6 months learning various skills to develop websites. Upon completion I set out to look for jobs near me. And thats when disaster struck.

The varst majority of jobs in my area want C# skills, so armed with this new knowledge I started taking the C#.NET course at Grand Circus. And we are now caught up to present day.
